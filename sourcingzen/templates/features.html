Features

  • Set up your private reverse auction

    When you need to purchase a product, you will be able to set up a private auction. In this way, your suppliers will compete in offering you the best deal at the lowest bid.

  • Invite only who you want

    Invite your trusted suppliers. Or invite new potential suppliers to see what is their best price. In any case, you decide who participates in your auction.

  • Make savings

    Realize savings by receiving better offers, but also by reducing the time required for a purchase. Finally, saving cost by using a web hosted solution, accessible from everywhere and available at affordable prices also for professionals and small companies.
